# CyberPath Academy — 30-Day Go-To-Market Plan

CyberPath Academy wins only if schools, cyber clubs, mentors, and beginner learners trust the safe defensive model enough to use it repeatedly and pay for cohort accountability. Do not add more content until the pilot funnel produces evidence.

## Week 1 — Polish Demo and Start Outreach

- Polish public demo so the school/cohort value is obvious in under 10 seconds.
- Prepare a school pilot pitch focused on safe fictional labs, mentor dashboards, student reports, CSV exports, and portfolio proof.
- Contact 20 students from schools, cyber clubs, tutoring centers, and beginner tech communities.
- Contact 5 teachers, mentors, or learning-center owners.
- Log every conversation with role, organization, city/country, need, objections, willingness to pay, and pilot interest.

## Week 2 — Student Tests and Feedback Loop

- Run 10 observed student tests without coaching.
- Collect feedback after onboarding, lessons, quizzes, labs, artifact creation, and demo exit.
- Ask what confused them, which lab felt useful, whether they would recommend it, and whether they or their school would pay.
- Fix the top 5 issues blocking comprehension, safety trust, or pilot conversion.
- Re-test fixes with at least 3 users.

## Week 3 — Mini Cohort

- Run a mini cohort with 10–20 learners.
- Require 3 defensive fictional labs and 1 proof-of-work artifact.
- Use the mentor dashboard to monitor activity, weak topics, lab submissions, portfolio artifacts, and students needing help.
- Export the cohort CSV and show it to a teacher or mentor.
- Interview 3 mentors or teachers about reporting, safety trust, classroom constraints, and buying process.

## Week 4 — Pilot Offers and First Package

- Offer a pilot to 3 schools or learning centers.
- Define the first paid package: cohort size, mentor seats, duration, support level, reporting cadence, and success criteria.
- Collect real testimonials only if earned through actual usage and explicit permission.
- Decide whether to continue, narrow, or pivot based on usage, interviews, and serious commitment.

## Metrics by Day 30

- 100 demo users.
- 30 feedback submissions.
- 10 interviews.
- 3 school pilot conversations.
- 1 paid or serious pilot commitment.

## Founder Operating Rule

If schools praise the idea but will not schedule a pilot, assign learners, review reports, or discuss budget, CyberPath is not yet painful enough. Narrow the segment before building more features.

# Privacy and School Safety

## Principles

- Collect the minimum data needed for learning and mentor support.
- Use fictional lab data; do not ask students to upload real secrets, credentials, private school records, or third-party personal data.
- Provide clear parent/school consent workflows before real classroom deployment.
- Separate demo data from real learner records.

## Checklist before school pilot

- Privacy policy reviewed by counsel or qualified advisor.
- Terms of use reviewed.
- School consent/DPA template prepared.
- Parent/guardian consent process defined where required.
- Data retention and deletion process implemented.
- Export process for student reports documented.
- Incident notification process documented.
- Teacher role boundaries and cohort access controls tested.

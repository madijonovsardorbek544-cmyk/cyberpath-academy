# 30-Day Beta Execution Plan

## Week 1: Small usability tests

- Recruit 10 students.
- Recruit 2 teachers or mentors.
- Run observed usability testing.
- Require each tester to attempt onboarding, one lesson, one practice set, one lab, and feedback.
- Fix the top 5 blockers only.

## Week 2: Student beta

- Run 10–20 student beta.
- Require 1 lesson, 1 practice set, 1 lab, and 1 artifact.
- Collect feedback after each major step.
- Track stuck users, abandoned pages, bug reports, and confusion themes.

## Week 3: Teacher/mentor review

- Run teacher or mentor dashboard review.
- Test cohort dashboard comprehension.
- Inspect weak topics, student reports, lab submissions, and artifact reviews.
- Improve reports and assignments only if teachers cannot understand them.

## Week 4: Pilot sales validation

- Pitch 3 schools, clubs, or learning centers.
- Offer first controlled pilot.
- Decide pricing experiment: per-student, per-cohort, or school/club license.
- Use willingness-to-pay feedback to decide whether to continue, reposition, or pause.

## Success metrics

- 30 users.
- 20 onboarding completions.
- 15 lesson completions.
- 10 lab submissions.
- 5 artifacts created.
- 20 feedback submissions.
- 3 teacher conversations.
- 1 serious school pilot lead.

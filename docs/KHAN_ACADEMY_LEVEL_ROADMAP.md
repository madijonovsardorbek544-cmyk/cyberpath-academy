# Khan Academy-Level Maturity Roadmap

## What is implemented now

- Defensive-only product positioning.
- Mock and backend learner flows.
- Skill tree and mastery MVP.
- Practice/review sessions and exercise catalog.
- Fictional labs and portfolio artifacts.
- Mentor/admin dashboards, pilot leads, feedback, and validation metrics.
- Build, backend tests, and mock smoke tests.

## What still needs 10x more work

- 10x more reviewed content and exercises.
- Real learner data to calibrate mastery.
- Teacher testing across multiple cohorts.
- Production monitoring, backups, restore drills, and incident readiness.
- Full localization, accessibility, and mobile testing.
- Content authoring/review tooling.
- School operations, procurement, consent, and support workflows.

## Milestones

- **M1 Stable beta:** E2E tests, 50 reviewed lessons, 20 labs, onboarding fixes, monitoring in staging.
- **M2 Skill tree + mastery MVP:** validated skill prerequisites, review debt, weak skill detection, student/teacher views.
- **M3 300 exercises:** all exercise types with explanations, hints, wrong-answer feedback, and safety validation.
- **M4 Teacher dashboard pilot:** assignment builder, printable reports, CSV exports, intervention notes.
- **M5 10-school pilot:** consent, onboarding, support, analytics, and teacher interviews.
- **M6 Production deployment:** managed DB, monitoring, backups, restore drills, legal docs, incident process.
- **M7 Full localization:** English/Uzbek complete; Russian or other languages clearly marked partial until complete.
- **M8 Real payment and school contracts:** provider checkout, invoicing, contracts, no raw card collection.
- **M9 1,000 active learners:** reliability, content analytics, support operations, cohort analytics.
- **M10 Khan Academy-level maturity candidate:** proven outcomes, accessibility, localization, massive content library, trusted school adoption.

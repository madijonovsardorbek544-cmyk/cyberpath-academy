# Khan Academy-Level Maturity Roadmap

_Last updated: 2026-05-16._

## What is implemented now

- Defensive-only product positioning.
- Mock and backend learner flows.
- Skill tree and mastery MVP with nine categories.
- Practice/review sessions and an exercise catalog.
- Backend seed minimum: 53 lessons, 318 quiz questions, 27 safe labs, 10 guided projects, and 157 glossary terms.
- Fictional labs and portfolio artifacts with rubric-style feedback.
- Mentor/admin dashboards with roster scope tests, heatmaps, lab/artifact review queues, assignment recommendations, and CSV export.
- Build, backend tests, and mock smoke tests.
- Localization honesty: English-only public beta, Uzbek/Russian marked review-only, with fallback and missing-key reporting smoke coverage.

## What still needs 10x more work

- 10x more reviewed lessons, exercises, labs, projects, diagrams, and remediation examples.
- Real browser E2E, mobile QA, accessibility audits, and cross-browser regression testing.
- Real learner outcome data to calibrate mastery and spaced review.
- Teacher testing across multiple cohorts with assignment lifecycle polish and reporting workflows.
- Production monitoring, backups, restore drills, incident readiness, and support operations.
- Full localization with accountable reviewers.
- Content authoring/review tooling with versioned publishing gates.
- School operations, procurement, consent, privacy, retention, and support workflows.

## Milestones

- **M1 Stable beta:** Playwright E2E unblocked, mobile/accessibility pass, monitoring in staging, moderated 10-learner pilot.
- **M2 Skill tree + mastery validation:** prerequisites, review debt, weak skill detection, and next-action recommendations validated with learner evidence.
- **M3 Reviewed item bank:** 1,000+ exercises with explanations, hints, wrong-answer feedback, and safety validation.
- **M4 Teacher dashboard pilot:** assignment lifecycle, printable reports, CSV exports, intervention notes, and mentor workflow interviews.
- **M5 10-school pilot:** consent, onboarding, support, analytics, and teacher interviews.
- **M6 Production deployment:** managed DB, monitoring, backups, restore drills, legal docs, and incident process.
- **M7 Full localization:** English/Uzbek complete; Russian or other languages clearly marked partial until complete.
- **M8 Real payment and school contracts:** provider checkout, invoicing, contracts, and no raw card collection.
- **M9 1,000 active learners:** reliability, content analytics, support operations, and cohort analytics.
- **M10 Khan Academy-level maturity candidate:** proven outcomes, accessibility, localization, massive content library, trusted school adoption.

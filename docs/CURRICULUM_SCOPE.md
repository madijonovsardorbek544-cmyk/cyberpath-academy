# Curriculum Scope

## Current scope

CyberPath Academy currently targets beginner defensive cybersecurity learners, schools, mentors, and cyber clubs. The product has a nine-category skill tree covering Cyber Foundations, Identity and Access, Networking for Cybersecurity, Linux and Systems Basics, Blue Team/SOC Foundations, Web Security Defense, Cloud and IAM Defense, GRC and Risk, and AI Security Awareness.

## Minimum credible beta scope

For a controlled beta, the platform should maintain at least:

- 50 reviewed lessons.
- 300 exercises across all target exercise types.
- 20 fictional safe labs.
- 10 guided projects.
- 120 glossary terms.
- Teacher-facing assignments, reports, exports, and intervention guidance.

## Non-goals

The curriculum must not teach offensive exploitation, credential theft, malware, evasion, persistence, phishing creation, unauthorized access, or real-target operations. Every lab must be toy/fictional and framed around defense, evidence, prevention, reporting, and authorized response.

# CyberPath Academy Product Strategy

CyberPath Academy is positioned as a defensive-only cybersecurity learning platform for beginner students, teenagers, schools, mentors, and emerging-market learners, especially Uzbek/English learners.

## Target users
- Beginner students who need a structured roadmap instead of scattered videos.
- Schools and learning centers that need safe practical cyber labs.
- Mentors who need progress visibility, weak-topic signals, assignments, and reports.
- Early career learners preparing for university, SOC/GRC/AppSec/cloud foundations, or portfolio review.

## Business model
- **Free:** limited beginner lessons, starter quizzes, starter labs, basic dashboard, and mistake notebook.
- **Premium:** all lessons/labs, guided tutor, certificates, guided projects, portfolio publishing, and full analytics.
- **School / Cohort:** Premium plus cohort dashboard, assignments, student reports, CSV exports, and cohort analytics.

## Current MVP moat
The strongest differentiator is proof-of-work: students can turn fictional safe labs into incident reports, access review memos, risk registers, secure-code summaries, and public read-only portfolio artifacts.

## Honest beta status
This is not a mature production platform yet. Before paid public launch, CyberPath needs school pilots, accessibility review, privacy/terms review, real payment provider integration, real email delivery, monitoring, backups, incident runbooks, and user validation.
